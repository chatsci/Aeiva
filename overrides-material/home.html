    A comprehensive platform for AI research

    We integrate various capabilities in Aeiva to help AI research in different domains. See below for more details.

    Get started

    A virtual assistant that makes your life better! ¶

    • Aeiva augments human intelligence

      We focus on developing artificial intelligence techniques that augment human intelligence rather than replace human.

    • Multimodal and embodied agent

      Aeiva aims to support multimodal and embodied environments. Currently, it can process text, image, audio, and video inputs.

    • AI for science

      Aeiva aims to be an expert agent that equipped with professional skills and can help accelerate scientific discovery. We currently focus on materials science and health.

    • Safe, controllable, and interpretable

      We emphasize on the safety, controllability, and interpretability (SCI) of AI. We aim to incorporate SCI techniques in Aeiva, as well as developing and evaluating SCI techniques based on it.

    • AI community and society

      We aim to construct multi-agent AI community and society for self-evolving AI.

    • Open source

      We open source our code on GitHub. Licensed under Apache license 2.0.
